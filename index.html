Our Story

A Kitchen Full of Love & Flour

Bakers Hub By AJ started from a single kitchen, a big dream, and an even bigger love for baking. Founded by AJ in 2018, we believe a cake isn't just a dessert — it's a memory waiting to happen.

Every recipe is crafted from scratch using fresh, quality ingredients. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just honest, soul-warming bakes that make every occasion sweeter.

From intimate birthday celebrations to grand weddings — we pour the same passion into every single order, big or small.
